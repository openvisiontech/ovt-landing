**Company Overview**

Open Vision Technology provides full-stack software solutions for robotic systems. Our flagship product, **Uli SDK**, serves as the "connective tissue" between physical infrastructure and Agentic AI. We enable connected assets to dynamically export data and capabilities as **AI Context**, allowing AI agents to discover, interact with, and control complex hardware through a unified, standardized interface.

**Core Competencies**

* **Agentic AI Interoperability:** Enabling autonomous agents to reason across diverse hardware fleets by abstracting machine functions into discoverable AI "tools" and “resources.”

* **A2UI (Agent-to-UI) Framework:** Native **Dart-FFI** integration for the Dart/Flutter ecosystem. Enables AI Agents to drive real-time, high-fidelity user interfaces and dashboarding directly from robotic telemetry streams.

* **AI Agent Skills-based Middleware:** First-in-class implementation of **Agent Skills** for robotics, ensuring zero-friction integration with LLMs and AI frameworks.

* **Dynamic Asset Discovery:** Real-time exportation of asset "agents" (tasks) and data streams, eliminating the need for hard-coded integrations.

* **Full-Stack Robotic Software:** Expertise in sensor fusion, teleoperation, and autonomous navigation (proven on our in-house 3-wheel test robot, **Uli Kaya**).

* **Legacy-to-AI Bridging:** Wrapping traditional industrial and defense infrastructures into modern, agent-consumable ecosystems.

**Differentiators**

* **Discovery-First Architecture:** Unlike static APIs, **Uli SDK** allows AI agents to query a system and "learn" what an asset can do on the fly.

* **Hardware Agnostic:** Designed to work across diverse platforms, from ground robotics (Uli Kaya) to fixed industrial sensors.

* **Reduced Integration Latency:** Standardized AI Agent Skills allow for rapid deployment of AI-driven command and control (C2) without custom middleware for every new asset.

**Past Performance / Use Cases**
